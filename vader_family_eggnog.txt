Vader Family Eggnog
From Donna, By way of Rebecca Vader, put into git by Meg

#dessert
#drink

This is a thinner eggnog than store bought. If you like thicker eggnog seperate the eggs and beat the whites.  


Ingredients:
2 Eggs
1 Can Sweetened Condensed Milk
2 Cups Milk, preferably whole milk
1 Tsp Vanilla
2 Cups Milk or Half and Half
1 Cup Cream, whipped
Freshly Grated Nutmeg

Instructions:
1. In the top of a double boiler, or on low heat in heavy bottom pan to avoid scorching, beat together
	a. 2 Eggs
	b. 1 Can Sweetened Condensed Milk
	c. 2 Cups (Whole) Milk
2. Place over boiling water and stir often until liquid reaches 140F.  Hold 1 minute to pasteurize eggs then pour into pitcher.  (This step can be skipped if you're not serving kids, the elderly, pregnant, or those in delicate health and you have VERY fresh eggs from a reliable source.)
3. Stir in
	a. 1 Tsp Vanilla
	b. 2 Cups Milk or Half and Half
	c. 1 Cup Cream, whipped
4. Chill 1 hour or until serving
5. Top with freshly grated nutmeg
