Pesto Caprese Sandwiches with an Almond-Studded Green Salad

#short
#veggie
#hellofresh

Servings: 2

Ingredients:
2 Ciabatta Rolls
3 oz Mixed Greens
4 oz Fresh Mozzarella
2 Roma Tomatos
5 teaspoon Balsamic Vinegar
1/2 oz Almonds
4 tablespoon pesto
2 teaspoon Olive Oil
Salt
Pepper

Instructions:
1. Wash and dry all produce. 
2. Halve ciabatta rolls. Toast until golden if desired. Thinly slice mozzarella into rounds. Halve tomatoes lengthwise; slice crosswise into half-moons.
3. In a medium bowl, toss mixed greens and almonds with vinegar and a large drizzle of olive oil. Season with salt and pepper.
4. Spread pesto onto cut sides of ciabatta rolls. Fill with tomatoes, mozzarella, and a bit of salad.
5. Halve sandwiches on a diagonal and divide between plates. Toss any remaining tomatoes into remaining salad; serve on the side.
